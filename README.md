# CampusConnect
# Software for IIITDM QnA community and Project Collaboration:
## Description:
Our objective is to establish a vibrant QnA community that serves as a hub for collaborative knowledge-sharing across diverse subjects. We aim to create an inclusive platform where individuals can freely ask questions, provide insightful answers, and exchange expertise. By fostering a culture of learning, our community strives to empower users with valuable information and build a collective repository of knowledge accessible to all.

## OBJECTIVES:

- QnA: Foster a Collaborative Knowledge-sharing Ecosystem
- Collaboration: To encourage team research and development cuture in our college


## ADVANTAGES OF ADOPTING THIS SOFTWARE:

1. Any student can ask questions relevant to existing fields/tags and get it answered by students or the faculty themselves
2. Users can ask any general questions(hostel, academic, elective choice, etc) and get the right responses by experienced people
3. Get involved in team projects and gain experience
4. Look for projects of your interests and join the team



## FEATURES:

1. ExchangeHub: Profile(Name, roll_no, reputation), Ask a question, Search questions based on fields/tags, View your questions/answers
2. Collaboration: Profile(Name, roll_no), Create a new collab(member limit, prereqs, experience), View open collabs

* User reputation: Indicates how trustworthy a user is

* Interaction with a question: upvote, downvote, report

* Feedback and suggestions: 
	- request for a new field(Ex: music, history) 
	- request for a new tag(DBMS, Web dev)
